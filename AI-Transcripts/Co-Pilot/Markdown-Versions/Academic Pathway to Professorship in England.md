# Next Steps Toward an Academic Career in England

## 1. Taught Master’s Degree (MSc/MRes)

- Purpose: deepen subject-specific and research skills ahead of a PhD.
- Duration: 1 year full-time (FT) or 2 years part-time (PT).
- Common Specialisms at Bradford:
  - Applied Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence MSc (FT, PT)  [bradford.ac.uk](https://www.bradford.ac.uk/postgraduate/taught-degrees/subjects/computing/)  
  - Artificial Intelligence and Space Technology MSc (FT, PT)  [bradford.ac.uk](https://www.bradford.ac.uk/postgraduate/taught-degrees/subjects/computing/)  
  - Cyber Security MSc (FT, PT)  [bradford.ac.uk](https://www.bradford.ac.uk/postgraduate/taught-degrees/subjects/computing/)  
  - Satellite Systems Engineering MSc (FT, PT)  [bradford.ac.uk](https://www.bradford.ac.uk/postgraduate/taught-degrees/subjects/computing/)  
- How to Apply: direct via Bradford’s postgraduate portal.  
- Typical Deadline: for October 2025 entry, aim to submit by end of August 2025.

---

## 2. Research Degree (PhD/MPhil)

- Purpose: develop original research—essential for lecturer and professorial roles.
- Duration: 3–4 years FT or up to 6 years PT.
- Options at Bradford:
  - PhD in Computer Science (projects in AI, cybersecurity, networks, etc.).  
  - MPhil by research.  
- How to Apply: direct to Bradford’s Research Degrees Office.  
- Funding & Deadlines: many doctoral scholarships close in January each year, so early-year applications (January–March) are recommended.

---

## 3. Higher Education Teaching Qualification (PGCert LTHE)

- Purpose: build pedagogical expertise, gain HEA accreditation for university teaching.
- Example Programme:
  - University of London – Postgraduate Certificate in Learning and Teaching in Higher Education  
    • Mode: online, part-time  
    • Duration: 1–5 years  
    • Intakes: October 2025 & April 2026  [london.ac.uk](https://www.london.ac.uk/study/courses/postgraduate/pgcert-learning-teaching-higher-education)  
- How to Apply: direct to the provider’s portal; check each course’s “Key dates” section (often 6–8 weeks before intake).

---

## 4. Postgraduate Teacher Training (PGCE via UCAS)

- Purpose: obtain Qualified Teacher Status (QTS) for secondary or primary teaching.
- Routes (all via UCAS Teacher Training service)  [ucas.com](https://www.ucas.com/postgraduate/teacher-training/postgraduate-teacher-training-england):
  - PGCE (university-led)  
  - School Direct (tuition-fee or salaried)  
  - Postgraduate Teaching Apprenticeship  
- Duration: typically 1 year FT.
- Application Deadlines:
  - Most courses advise applying by January for September start.  
  - Final deadline for late applications is usually late June, but check individual provider.

## Final Tips

- Taught and research postgraduate courses (MSc, MRes, PhD) are applied to directly via each university, not through UCAS undergraduate.
- Always consult Bradford’s own course pages for up-to-date deadlines, entry requirements and fee-status details.
- For PhD funding, identify supervisors early and align your application with scholarship deadlines (often January).
- Completing a PGCert LTHE or HEA fellowship bolsters your teaching portfolio when applying for academic posts.
- If you need QTS for a stint in secondary teaching, UCAS Teacher Training is your one-stop portal.  

Embarking on this sequence—master’s, doctorate, plus a recognised HE teaching qualification—will position you strongly for a lectureship and, in time, an associate professorship at the University of Bradford.
