# Challenges for EA FC 25 Manager Mode  

## Me
I'm playing EA Fc25 Manager Mode where I'm going to create my own club in the Portuguese league. What are some interesting challenges i can put on the wheel to spin that can be completed after the career mode has started and been setup?  

## Co-Pilot
That sounds like a lot of fun! Here are some interesting challenges you can add to your wheel for EA FC 25 Manager Mode:

1. **Promote Three Youth Players**: Sign a few high-potential youth players and develop them until they're ready for the senior team.[1]
2. **Win a Major Competition**: Aim to win the league, cup, or even the Champions League with your club.[1]
3. **Make Five Top Grade Transfers**: Secure five high-quality transfers with an A grade in the Transfer Rating.[1]
4. **Hire a 5-Star Coach**: Bring in a top-tier coach to improve your team's performance.[1]
5. **Complete 20 Training Drills**: Focus on improving your team's skills through various training drills.[1]
6. **Participate in 10 Press Conferences**: Engage with the media and handle press conferences to build your club's reputation.[1]
7. **Win a Title with a Weak Team**: Take charge of a weaker team and aim to win a title, which can be very rewarding.[2]
8. **Develop a Home-Grown Squad**: Focus on developing and promoting youth players to eventually replace the entire squad with home-grown talent.[2]
9. **Achieve a Top-Four Finish in the League**: Aim for a consistent performance and secure a spot in the top four of the league.
10. **Win a Rush Tournament with Youth Players**: Compete in bi-monthly Rush tournaments using your club's youth players.[2]
