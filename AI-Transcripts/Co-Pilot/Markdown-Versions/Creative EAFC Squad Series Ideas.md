# Creative EAFC Squad Series Ideas  

## Me
In my spare time I make FIFA (now EAFC) squads using different cards and players based on a theme and screenshot them then. Sometimes I do different series and I want to start a new one now. I had an idea in mind but can't seem to remember it now. Based on the series I've done in the past, can you give me some ideas for the new series, bearing in mind that female players are now in the game as well and there could be some interesting things with them?  

Past series:  
- Random Positions - Took an irl team and randomised the positions of their top players
- One man, many teams - Took a player who's played for many different clubs in their career and included different cards from all their past clubs
- Perfect links - Made a squad of combinations of players from different leagues who play for the same club and nation (2 or 3 player combos from different teams and nations to have 4 or 5 different combos in the full starting 11)
- Best all-time teams - All the best players in history that played for a certain club
- Natives - Players that currently play in their country's native league or vice versa (non-natives included separately as part of this series as well) e.g. Spanish players who play in La Liga, English players who play in the premier league or Players from all different countries who play in the premier league for non-natives, etc.
- Random squads - Squads of players taken from a randomiser (each player in the squad is randomised separately)
- ToTDs - The best players from a certain club in the 2010-2019 decade
- Throwback icons - Based on the throwback icons card - a legend of the game shown at one of their top previous clubs - a full team of popular icons shown on their old clubs

### ⚽ **New Series Ideas for EAFC (with Female Players)**

#### 1. **Mixed Legacy XI**
- **Concept**: Combine male and female legends from the same club or nation.
- **Example**: A Brazil squad with Pelé, Marta, Ronaldinho, Formiga, etc.
- **Twist**: Highlight how both sides of the game shaped football history.

#### 2. **Dual Dynasties**
- **Concept**: Pick a club that has strong men’s and women’s teams and build a squad mixing stars from both.
- **Example**: Chelsea (Sam Kerr, Fran Kirby, Reece James, Eden Hazard).
- **Bonus**: Could do a “battle of dynasties” format—Chelsea vs. Barcelona, etc.

#### 3. **Career Crossroads**
- **Concept**: Players who’ve had similar career paths across genders.
- **Example**: Male and female players who both played for Lyon, PSG, and Barcelona.
- **Why it works**: Shows parallel journeys and club influence across both games.

#### 4. **Sibling Styles**
- **Concept**: Match male and female players with similar play styles or positions.
- **Example**: Leah Williamson and Virgil van Dijk as ball-playing CBs.
- **Fun angle**: You could even rate chemistry based on style, not just links.

#### 5. **Global Icons: Gender Edition**
- **Concept**: Build squads of the most iconic male and female players from each continent.
- **Example**: Europe XI, South America XI, Africa XI, North America XI.
- **Twist**: Could do “past vs. present” versions too.

#### 6. **Club & Country Clash**
- **Concept**: Players who play for a club in one country but represent another internationally.
- **Example**: Wendie Renard (France, Lyon), Khadija Shaw (Jamaica, Man City).
- **Why it’s cool**: Highlights global movement and national pride.

#### 7. **FIFA Firsts**
- **Concept**: Squad of players who were the first from their country, gender, or club to achieve something in FIFA/EAFC.
- **Example**: First female Ballon d'Or winner, first player with 99 pace, etc.
- **Bonus**: Could include milestone cards or special editions.

#### 8. **Chemistry Chaos**
- **Concept**: Build squads with max chemistry using only obscure links—like same birthday, same youth academy, or same shirt number.
- **Twist**: Use both male and female players to make it even trickier.

#### 9. **Generational Talent**
- **Concept**: Pick a birth year (e.g. 1997) and build a squad of players born that year—male and female.
- **Why it works**: You can compare how talent develops across genders and leagues.

#### 10. **Themed Duos**
- **Concept**: Each pair in the squad shares a theme—e.g. both captains, both top scorers, both known for long shots.
- **Example**: Alex Morgan & Harry Kane (clinical finishers), Bronze & Lahm (defensive leaders).
