# Email Template for University Admissions Inquiry  

## Me
My cousin has studied in Pakistan and is now looking to pursue a Masters degree here in England and also bring a dependent (spouse). How can I send an email to my university admissions team?  

## Co-Pilot
That sounds like an exciting step for your cousin! To reach out to the university admissions team, your email should be clear and professional. Here's a template to get started:

---

**Subject:** Inquiry Regarding Master's Admission and Dependent Visa  

Dear [Admissions Team],  

I hope this email finds you well. My cousin, [Cousin's Full Name], has completed their studies in Pakistan and is keen to pursue a Master’s degree at [University Name]. They are particularly interested in [Specific Course Name] and would like guidance on the admission requirements, deadlines, and any additional documents needed.  

Additionally, they plan to bring their spouse as a dependent. Could you please provide information on visa eligibility, financial requirements, and any university-related support for dependents?  

We would greatly appreciate any details or resources you could share regarding this process. Please let us know the best way to proceed with the application.  

Thank you for your time and assistance.  

Best regards,  
[Your Name]  
[Your Contact Information]  

---

You can personalize this based on specific details about the university and course. Feel free to add any additional questions!
