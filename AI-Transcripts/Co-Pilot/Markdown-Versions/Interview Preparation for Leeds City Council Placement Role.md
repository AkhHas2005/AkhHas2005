**Hands-On Demonstration:**  
- While it may not be a live coding test, interviewers often ask you to discuss how you’d troubleshoot an issue or optimize an environment. Practice articulating your process clearly and logically.

These practised scenarios show that you’re not only theoretically sound but also practically ready to manage real-world challenges.
